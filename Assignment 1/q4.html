Engineering Course

Course Objectives

The objective of the Engineering Course is to equip students with the necessary technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and knowledge required to excel in various fields of engineering, including mechanical, electrical, civil, and computer science.

Fees

The course fee for the Engineering program is $10,000 per year.

Duration

The duration of the Engineering course is 4 years.

Management Course

Course Objectives

The objective of the Management Course is to develop leadership, decision-making, and strategic thinking skills in students. It aims to prepare individuals for roles in business, finance, marketing, and human resource management.

Fees

The course fee for the Management program is $8,000 per year.

Duration

The duration of the Management course is 2 years.
